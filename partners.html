Our Partners

Partnering with pioneers to power the present and future of esports.

Dragon Streak empowers the world's most forward-thinking brands to build authentic relevance in esports. Today, these same partners are extending their support to the Dragon Streak Academy, helping to shape the next generation of talent. Discover how our collaborative partnerships drive innovation and create lasting impact—and explore how you can be a part of it.

It speaks volumes about our shared vision with our main partner and sponsor extends its support beyond the main stage to the Dragon Streak Academy. As a leading cryptocurrency trading and investment platform, their commitment does more than just fund our future—it actively shapes it. This partnership infuses the Academy with cutting-edge financial technology insights, allowing our students to grasp the complex economic landscape of modern esports. Their involvement ensures our aspiring professionals are not just skilled players, but savvy, forward-thinking pioneers equipped for the next frontier of competitive gaming.

Having Urbann Automotive AS join us as a sponsor of the Dragon Streak Academy is a powerful testament to our shared commitment to innovation and the future. As a pioneering Norwegian company crafting small, intelligent electric city cars, Urbann embodies the principles of smart design and sustainable progress. This partnership is about more than support; it’s about aligning values. Just as Urbann is redefining urban mobility with clean, efficient technology, we are nurturing the next generation of esports talent. Their involvement brings a crucial, real-world perspective on sustainability and cutting-edge technology to our students, helping shape competitors who are as strategic and forward-thinking about their careers as Urbann is about shaping the future of transportation.
